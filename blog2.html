Success Story of Flipkart

Title description, Sep 18, 2020

Sachin Bansal & Binny Bansal The Walmart acquired ecommerce company Flipkart stands as the largest ecommerce platform in India. Today, the ecommerce platform has expanded its business horizons by foraying into the video streaming industry with Flipkart Video Originals.

Sachin Bansal & Binny Bansal started out as an online bookstore that made around 20 successful shipments in its first year of functioning. It was not late before the platform started grabbing people’s attention that made it the top online retail market in history.

Fact: The first customer of Flipkart was a young engineer from Mahbubnagar, Telangana.

Success Story of Paytm

Title description, Sep 18, 2020

Vijay Shekhar Sharma, Paytm started out as a digital wallet in its initial days but has transformed into a completely new payments platform. The fintech service has been able to emerge as the top fintech company even after facing fierce early competition from brands like Freecharge.

Vijay Shekhar Sharma, who was struggling to make ends meet with Rs 10 in his pocket, tasted victory the hard way, and today, he stands as the founder of the billion-dollar homegrown unicorn

Fact: Paytm’s userbase grew from 125 million to 185 million three months post demonetization.
